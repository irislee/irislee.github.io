Avi's Hierarchy
Oct 18th, 2013

Where you should be spending your time when working on a project:

  • Views: things like erb templates have the least priority.
  • Controllers: site generators, parsers, etc are second to least important.
  • Models: very high priority! Be sure to have a bunch of tests for your models.
  • Databases: super important to have a good foundation for your project so you can use it.
